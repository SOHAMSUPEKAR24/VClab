Experiment 8: Electroplating Copper on Iron Plate

Precautions:

  • Wear protective gloves and goggles to avoid contact with chemicals.
  • Ensure the copper sulfate solution is prepared properly.
  • Maintain the temperature of the solution as recommended (usually 25°C to 30°C).
  • Ensure the power supply is working correctly to apply a constant current.

Instructions:

This experiment involves electroplating copper onto an iron plate. Please follow these steps:

  1. Immerse the iron plate in the copper sulfate solution.
  2. Connect the iron plate to the cathode and a copper rod to the anode.
  3. Set current to 0.5A and apply for 30 minutes.
  4. Observe the copper coating on the iron plate after the process completes.
