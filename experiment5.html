Experiment 5: Extraction of Lignin from Lignocellulosic Biomass

Precautions:

  • Always wear appropriate protective equipment such as gloves, goggles, and lab coat.
  • Work in a well-ventilated area or under a fume hood when handling NaOH solution.
  • Ensure that the heating process is done using a controlled heat source to avoid overheating.
  • Dispose of waste chemicals properly according to your institution’s guidelines.
  • Handle all chemicals with care and follow safety protocols for handling caustic solutions.

Instructions:

To begin lignin extraction, follow the steps below:

  1. Place 20g of biomass sample into the reactor.
  2. Add 200mL of 10% NaOH solution.
  3. Heat the mixture at 90°C for 2 hours.
  4. Cool, filter, and collect lignin. Record the yield.
