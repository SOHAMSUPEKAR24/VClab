Experiment 1: Determine Strength of Strong Acid Using pH Metry

Precautions:

  • Always wear protective gear, including gloves, goggles, and a lab coat.
  • Handle strong acids with care and avoid direct contact with skin.
  • Ensure proper ventilation when handling acids to avoid inhaling fumes.
  • In case of skin contact, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical attention.
  • Dispose of all acid solutions properly, following the lab's disposal guidelines.

Instructions:

Follow the steps below to determine the strength of the acid using pH metry:

  1. Enter the volume of the acid (in mL).
  2. Enter the concentration of the acid (in M).
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to compute the pH of the solution based on the concentration.
