Experiment 3: Determine Hardness of Water Using EDTA

Precautions:

  • Wear appropriate protective equipment (gloves, goggles).
  • Handle EDTA solution with care and follow safety protocols.
  • Ensure accurate measurement of water sample volume and EDTA concentration.
  • Dispose of chemicals properly as per safety guidelines.

Instructions:

Follow the steps below to determine the hardness of the water sample:

  1. Measure the volume of the water sample in milliliters (mL).
  2. Measure the concentration of EDTA solution in moles per liter (M).
  3. Use the formula: Hardness = Volume (mL) × Concentration (M) × 1000 to calculate hardness.
