Experiment 4: Determine Alkalinity of Water

Precautions:

  • Wear appropriate protective equipment (gloves, goggles, etc.).
  • Handle all chemicals with care.
  • Ensure accurate measurement of titrant volume and concentration.
  • Dispose of waste chemicals properly as per safety guidelines.

Instructions:

Follow the steps below to determine the alkalinity of the water sample:

  1. Measure the titrant volume in milliliters (mL).
  2. Measure the concentration of the titrant solution in moles per liter (M).
  3. Use the formula: Alkalinity = Volume (mL) × Concentration (M) × 50 to calculate alkalinity.
